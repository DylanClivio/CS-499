Welcome to Dylan's Life "Organizer".

Appointments are events you plan in the long-term, like doctors visits, work meetings, and things like that!

Tasks are things you do in the short-term, like taking the dog out for a walk or hitting the gym!

Utilizing Java, MySQL, Tomcat, HTML, this webpage is a user-friendly way to manage all your life's appointments and tasks.

Just enter your appointment or task down below and it will be saved to the Database forever!

Just make sure to remember the ID and you'll be able to find all your saved tasks and appointments in the searches below!

This tool can be used for all kinds of storage so use it anyway that's most convenient for you!
